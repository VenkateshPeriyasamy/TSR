REFRESHER PERSONAL SURVIVAL TECHNIQUES (PST)

Course duration 1 Day

Course Date Every Tuesday & Thursday of
the 1st 3 Week of Month

Fees 2,100 /-

prospectus

application form

ELIGIBILITY

  • All seafarers or prospective seafarers are eligible.
  • STCW 95/2010 Convention PST Certificate.
  • Certificate of fitness from a Registered Medical Practitioner (Certificate should not be more than one month old on date of commencement of course).
  • 3 Passport size photographs.
  • INDOS No.

SPECIAL INSTRUCTION

  • As candidates will be under going wet drills in the swimming pool, they must bring their towel, inner wear and change of clothes with them.

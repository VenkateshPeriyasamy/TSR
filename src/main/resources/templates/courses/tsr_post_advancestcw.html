GMDSS (INDIAN)

Course duration 2 Weeks

Course Date Every Month
(Schedule Attached)

Fees 22,000/- (+ Service
Tax as applicable)

prospectus

application form

ELIGIBILITY

  • Be a citizen of India*
  • Be not less than 18 yrs on the date of examination.
  • Have passed HSC/ISC (10 + 2) examination with Physics and Mathematics as main subjects OR - have a valid Certificate of Competency issued by the D.G Shipping OR Have valid Certificate of Proficiency issued by Ministry of Communication (WPC Wing).
  • Have undergone 2 weeks practical training on GMDSS equipment in an Institute approved by the Ministry of Communications, New Delhi.
  • Have completed and submitted an application form for GOI examination along with stipulated fees and passport size photographs.
  • * Note : - Non-Indian citizens can also be admitted provided they fulfill all other eligibility requirements described above and in addition have 1) valid passport and visa issued by the appropriate authorities and 2) are not less than 18 years on the date of examination.

FAQ's

  • Course duration
  • Residential or Non residential
  • Transport arrangement
  • Family accommodation

SPECIAL INSTRUCTION

  • It is important that candidates should get their documents (10+2) PCM mark sheet and Passport both original and attested when they join the course.
